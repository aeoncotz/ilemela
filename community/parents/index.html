Parents

We recognize parents as vital partners in the educational journey. We believe that strong collaboration between home and school builds a solid foundation for each child’s academic success and personal development.

Active Involvement

We encourage parents to be actively involved in school life. From attending meetings and school events to volunteering in classroom activities and special programs, parental presence greatly enhances the school experience for all learners. Your time, support, and feedback are valued and impactful.

Support and Resources

We also offer guidance and resources to help parents support learning at home, including tips for reading, homework help, and behavior support. Our school staff is always available to listen and assist when needed.

Parent Committees

Parents have the opportunity to participate in school committees that support events, fundraising, health and welfare, and educational improvement. These groups foster a sense of ownership and unity within our school community.

Working Together

When parents and schools work together with mutual respect and shared goals, children thrive. We are proud of the strong parent community we’ve built and look forward to continuing our partnership in shaping confident, capable, and caring learners.
