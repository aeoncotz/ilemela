Accreditations

We are fully registered and accredited by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology under the Government of the United Republic of Tanzania. We adhere strictly to the national education policies and curriculum guidelines set forth by the Tanzania Institute of Education (TIE).

Our school delivers the Tanzanian Primary School Curriculum, which emphasizes core competencies in Literacy, Numeracy, Science, Social Studies, Civics, Vocational Skills, Information and Communication Technology (ICT), and Life Skills.

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

We follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) approach, which promotes learner-centered teaching, critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving. Assessments are both continuous and summative, aligned with standards set by the National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA) to ensure learners are well-prepared for the Primary School Leaving Examination (PSLE).

Being a recognized institution, our pupils are eligible to participate in all national assessments, examinations, and educational activities. We are proud to uphold the values of quality, discipline, and academic integrity in accordance with Tanzanian private education regulations.
