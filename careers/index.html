Careers

We are committed to academic excellence, character building, and nurturing young minds. Our staff plays a vital role in shaping the future, and we are always looking for passionate, qualified, and dedicated individuals to join our growing team.


Why Work With Us?

We offer a supportive, respectful, and purpose-driven environment where every staff member is valued. At our school, you'll find:

  • A commitment to professional growth and training
  • A collaborative team of educators and administrators
  • Opportunities to contribute meaningfully to students’ lives
  • A positive atmosphere grounded in discipline and Christian values

Who We Look For?

We welcome applications from:

  • Qualified teachers with experience in nursery or primary education
  • Administrative staff with school-based experience
  • Support staff who are dedicated, responsible, and student-focused
  • Individuals with a strong work ethic, good communication skills, and a heart for education
