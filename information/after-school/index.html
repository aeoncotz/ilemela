After School

Ensuring our students’ safety beyond the classroom, we offer a caring, watchful space where young minds thrive peacefully until they journey home safely aboard our trusted school buses.

Transportation by School Buses

All pupils are transported home exclusively by school buses, following designated routes covering the community. Our trained staff ensure supervised boarding and safe drop-off to authorized guardians. This service guarantees reliable, secure travel for every child.

Parents are encouraged to keep contact and pickup information up to date to maintain smooth operations.

Safety and Supervision

Throughout the after-school period, pupils are closely monitored by responsible staff in a caring and disciplined environment. Safety protocols are strictly followed to protect each learner until they reach home safely.

Snacks and Refreshments

Students receive light, nutritious snacks and clean drinking water to stay energized while waiting for departure.

Activities Provided after school
  • Homework support and study time
  • Reading, storytelling, and creative exercises
  • Arts, crafts, and indoor/outdoor play
  • Music, dance, and spiritual enrichment
  • Quiet time for rest and reflection
Operating Hours
  • Monday to Friday
  • From school dismissal (2:30 PM or 12:30 PM for nursery) until departure by bus
  • Pupils remain under careful adult supervision until safely boarded on school buses.
Commitment to Families

By providing comprehensive after-school care coupled with secure transportation, we ensure every child is cared for beyond the classroom, allowing parents peace of mind and confidence in our school’s commitment to their child’s safety and well-being.
