Uniform

Our uniform represents unity, discipline, and pride in our learning environment. Wearing the school uniform daily helps foster a sense of belonging, equality, and school identity among our pupils.


Formal School Shirt

Sky Blue & Plaid Detail

A crisp, short-sleeved button-down shirt featuring the official embroidered crest. Includes a unique plaid-lined inner collar and matching necktie.

Trousers & Shorts

Custom Navy/Green Plaid

Durable, tailored bottoms featuring a structured waistband and professional pleated front. Designed for comfort and style.

Official Sweater

Deep Navy V-Neck

Classic knit sweater with the full-color crest. Accented with signature light blue horizontal stripes for warmth and identity.

Casual Polo T-Shirt

Navy & Cream

Breathable polo for less formal activities. Features a cream-colored collar, tipped sleeves, and a functional chest pocket with the school seal.

Tracksuit Jacket

Royal Navy & White

High-performance athletic jacket with "three-stripe" detailing. Designed to keep students comfortable while staying active.
