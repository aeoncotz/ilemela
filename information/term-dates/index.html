Term Dates

The school’s academic calendar details term dates, holidays, and important events. Helps our parents and pupils stay informed and prepared throughout the school year for a smooth and successful learning experience.

Academic Terms
  • Term 1: Early January – Late May
  • Term 2: Early July – Late November

Each term is designed to cover specific syllabus content with regular assessments to monitor pupils progress.

School Holidays
  • Mid-Term Breaks: Short breaks between terms to allow rest and family time.
  • National Holidays: Observed according to the Tanzanian government calendar.
  • End-of-Year Holiday: Begins after Term 2 and lasts until the start of the next academic year.
Important Dates
  • Orientation Day: The first day of Term 1 for new and returning students.
  • Parent-Teacher Meetings: Scheduled each term to discuss student progress and collaboration.
  • Examination Weeks: Announced at the start of each term, covering continuous assessments and national exams.
  • School Events: Including sports days, cultural celebrations, and special assemblies.
Note to Parents

Please ensure your child attends school promptly on the first day of each term. Timely attendance helps maintain continuity in learning and supports academic success. Updates to the calendar are communicated through official notices and newsletters.
