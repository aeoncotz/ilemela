Lunch

We provide healthy, balanced lunches to support each learner’s growth, energy, and concentration. Meals are prepared safely, served hygienically, and enjoyed in a friendly, supervised environment that encourages good eating habits.

Nutritious and Balanced Meals

We serve freshly prepared meals made from clean, high-quality ingredients. Each meal includes a variety of food groups — carbohydrates, proteins, vegetables, and fruits — ensuring children receive the energy and nutrients they need.

Meals may include:
  • Rice, ugali, or chapati
  • Beans, lentils, meat, or eggs
  • Cooked vegetables and seasonal fruits
  • Safe, clean drinking water is provided daily
Hygiene and Safety

All meals are prepared by trained kitchen staff under strict hygiene and safety standards. Our kitchen is regularly inspected, and food is served in clean utensils. Handwashing is encouraged before and after eating.

Mealtime Routine

Lunch is served during the school day at a designated time, allowing students to eat calmly and comfortably. Mealtime also teaches children responsibility, table manners, and social interaction with their peers.

Special Dietary Needs

If a student has allergies or dietary restrictions, parents are encouraged to notify the school administration so appropriate arrangements can be made.

Parental Support

We thank our parents for supporting the lunch program by ensuring timely payment of meal fees and encouraging healthy eating habits at home.
