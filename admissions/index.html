Addmissions

We warmly invite new learners to become part of our caring and vibrant community, where every child is nurtured to grow academically, socially, and morally with strong values, knowledge, and confidence.

Who Can Apply

We accept applications for :
  • Nursery Level: Children aged 3 years and above.
  • Primary Level: Standard One to Standard Seven.

All applicants must meet the age and readiness criteria appropriate for their level, and parents/guardians must submit required documentation during the application period.


Admission Requirements

To apply, parents or guardians should provide:

  • A completed application form
    (available at the school office)
  • A copy of the child’s birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-size photos
  • Previous school report (If transferring)
  • Medical report or Immunization record


Interview and Placement

Placement is based on age, performance during the assessment (if applicable), and availability of space. We aim to ensure that each learner is placed in a level where they can thrive academically and socially.
