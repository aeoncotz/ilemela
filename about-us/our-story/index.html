Our Story

Our story began in 2013 with a vision to nurture young minds through quality education. Since then, Ilemela English Medium School has grown with purpose, passion, and a commitment to excellence.

Over time, the school slowly grew to a Kindergarten, then Standard One and by the turn of the years, a full primary school was established. From this point, the school has catered for pupils from Prep One to Standard Seven.

Our Priority

Considering the fact that a healthy school leaning environment is not a luxury but anecessity for the holistic development of students, our school strives to achieve this by prioritizing physical and mental well- being, embracing diversity, and fostering an inclusive culture.

Who we are

We are an accredited school providing an outstanding Tanzanian education. Academic achievement is at the heart of the school. Your child will find an atmosphere of purposeful curiosity, essential for developing the love of learning that will propel them forward in the world they will enter
