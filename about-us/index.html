About Us

Welcome to Ilemela English Medium School. Nestled in the heart of Mwanza, we warmly nurture young learners with quality education, strong values, and a joyful path toward a bright future.

Message from the School Director

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Visitors

It is with great joy that I welcome you to Ilemela English Medium School. Here, we believe that every child is a unique gift with great potential waiting to be nurtured. Our role is to provide a strong educational foundation rooted in knowledge, discipline, and values that prepare pupils for a bright and meaningful future.

At Ilemela, we are committed to creating a safe, engaging, and supportive environment where learners can thrive academically, socially, and spiritually. With the dedication of our qualified teachers and the cooperation of our parents, we continue to grow young leaders who are confident, respectful, and ready to make a positive difference in the world.

David Msanii
School Director


Vision

Finding out a better way we can train pupils to serve We provides quality education, guiding children to grow with confidence, respect, and responsibility for future success.

At Ilemela English Medium School, we envision a complete learning journey that begins in early childhood and grows through every stage of academic life. We aim to nurture confident, disciplined, and value-driven learners prepared to face tomorrow with courage.

Starting with nursery and primary education, our goal is to expand into ordinary and advanced secondary levels. Through this growth, we are building a strong, continuous path of excellence that shapes responsible leaders ready to serve and transform their communities.
